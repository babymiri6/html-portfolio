Movie Diaries of MiMi

Movies I Would Die For😘

1. Blood And Water

A teenage Netflix Series that tells a story of a how a teenager lived in the shadow of her "dead" older sister.
I recommend you watch if you want a blend of romance and investigative vibes 🧐

2. Leo

Leo is a 78 year old chameleon who strives to find a way of escaping his cage life, to enjoy the outside world.
A movie with a lot of inside jokes for you to lyao 🤣

3. Jumanji

Spencer and his friends get trapped in a video game and have to fight for their lives by playing the game to win.
The game avatars are movie stars like "The Rock" and Kevin Hart.
You're gonna love it 😍
